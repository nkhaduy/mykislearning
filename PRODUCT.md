# Product

## Register

product

## Users

KIS Vietnam employees completing assigned internal training, and HR/L&D staff assigning courses, monitoring evidence, and handling exceptions.

## Product Purpose

MyKIS Learning is the internal LMS for structured learning, assessment, transparent browser-scoped activity tracking, and auditable training administration.

## Brand Personality

Trustworthy, calm, precise. It should feel like a Korean securities product: disciplined and professional without becoming an administrative spreadsheet for learners.

## Anti-references

Avoid noisy admin dashboards, excessive boxed metrics, decorative motion, generic SaaS gradients, mixed-language UI, and punitive proctoring language.

## Design Principles

1. Keep the learner oriented around the next meaningful action.
2. Derive progress from evidence, never arbitrary input.
3. Explain locks and tracking rules in plain language.
4. Preserve familiar controls and quiet visual hierarchy.
5. Make exceptional HR actions explicit and auditable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA, full keyboard operation, visible focus, text alternatives, reduced motion, non-color status cues, captions/transcript alternatives, and no audio-only completion barrier.
